# Brand and Content Guide

## Design Concept: Himalayan Precision

The MokXya Hisab pre-launch website is built around the "Himalayan Precision" design concept. 
It must feel:
- Calm
- Trustworthy
- Precise
- Premium
- Intelligent
- Professional
- Nepal-aware
- Modern
- Accessible

### Typography
- **Primary:** 'Inter' or system-ui fallback.
- **Devanagari Support:** 'Noto Sans Devanagari', 'Mangal', 'Kalimati'.

### Color Palette
- **Refined Deep Blue:** Primary brand and CTAs (`#0d284a`).
- **Restrained Teal:** Accents and secondary actions (`#0f766e`).
- **Surfaces:** Neutral white and grey surfaces.
- **Dark Mode:** Deep slate backgrounds (`#0f172a` to `#1e293b`) with vibrant blue and teal.

### What NOT to Use
- Purple AI gradients
- Neon colors
- Glassmorphism
- Excessive rounded cards
- Rainbow feature cards
- Pale-green legacy accounting design

## Tone and Voice
- Simple English with carefully selected Nepali headings.
- Plain business language (no complex accounting jargon unless explained).
- Honest product claims (do not claim to replace an accountant).
